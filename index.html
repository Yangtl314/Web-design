Work

「Nyctophilic Narrations」

Genesis of the Chronicles

During a witching-hour recording session, the silhouette of an ancient scholar tree merged with childhood memories of Grandma's "Mountain Djinn Lantern" folktale... Discovering that epinephrine surges from controlled fear neutralize amygdala hyperactivity—thus born these series: A psychosomatic paradox where goosebumps become neural release valves.

「Nocturnal Reveries」

Genesis of the Chronicles

While sketching the Silk Road frescoes, the nine-tailed vulpecula's cloud patterns began humming at 174Hz—birthing the Oneiric Zephyr chronicles through ear canal caresses in hypnagogic states. Alchemizing Suzu bell harmonics with dew collects into tactile-tingling phonetics, crafting the ASMR equivalent of digitally caressing the Kitsune's tail.
